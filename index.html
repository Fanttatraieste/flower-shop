About us

  • Our flower shop is a family business that has been passed down through three generations. Our current owner initially pursued a career in professional sports, but upon retiring at the age of 30, he rediscovered his passion for flowers and decided to continue the family legacy. Despite his attempts to stray from his destiny, he found that his roads always led him back to the flower shop. Under his leadership, our business has expanded internationally across Europe, and we continue to take pride in providing the highest quality flowers to our customers
