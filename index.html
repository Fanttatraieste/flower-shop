About us

  • Our flower shop is a family business that has been passed down through three generations. Our current owner initially pursued a career in professional sports, but upon retiring at the age of 30, he rediscovered his passion for flowers and decided to continue the family legacy.

  • Looking towards the future, we have big dreams for our flower shop. Currently, we have 10 shops in 6 countries and no proper delivery system. However, we aspire to reach every corner of Europe and expand our business to at least 25 countries, with 3-4 flower shops in every country.

  • At our flower shop, we believe that every customer is an old friend waiting to be made. We never needed to advertise our services, as the word-of-mouth recommendations from our satisfied customers have been more than enough to sustain our business.
